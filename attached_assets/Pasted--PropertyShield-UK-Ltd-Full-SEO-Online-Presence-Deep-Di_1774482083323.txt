## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

PropertyShield UK Ltd has a **well-built website** with strong technical foundations — proper meta tags, structured data, HTTPS, mobile-friendly design, and a sitemap. However, the business is **virtually invisible online** in all the places that actually generate leads. The site has **near-zero Google indexation** (only 1 old page indexed), **no verified Google Business Profile appearing in search**, **zero presence on any trade directory** (Checkatrade, TrustATrader, MyBuilder, Yell — none), and social media accounts that can't be found via search. Meanwhile, 15-20 competitors are actively ranking for every Darlington roofing keyword.

The good news: the website itself is solid. The fix is about **getting it seen** — directory listings, Google Business optimization, reviews, and location pages.

## 1. WEBSITE TECHNICAL SEO — WHAT'S WORKING

### Strong Points
- **HTTPS** fully working with proper redirects (www → non-www)
- **Unique title tags & meta descriptions** on every page (homepage, 8 service pages, about, gallery, reviews, areas, contact)
- **Open Graph & Twitter cards** properly configured with 1200×630 og-image
- **Schema.org structured data** — `RoofingContractor` JSON-LD with correct business details, service areas (11 towns), opening hours
- **Heading structure** — single H1 per page, logical H2/H3 hierarchy
- **Internal linking** — every service page linked from homepage, strong CTA links
- **Sitemap** at `/sitemap.xml` with 16 URLs and priorities
- **robots.txt** allowing all crawlers
- **Mobile viewport** correctly configured
- **Cookie consent** with GDPR-compliant accept/reject
- **Favicon system** — complete with all 13 branded icon sizes

### Issues Found

#### CRITICAL
1. **Near-zero Google indexation** — Only 1 page indexed (the old /faq/ page). Homepage, service pages, about, contact — NONE are in Google yet
2. **SPA rendering limitation** — Site is React SPA. The SSR fallback in index.html only covers homepage content. Service pages have no server-rendered HTML for crawlers

#### HIGH PRIORITY
3. **No `<lastmod>` dates in sitemap.xml** — Google needs these for crawl scheduling
4. **Privacy page missing from sitemap**
5. **No FAQPage schema** — homepage has FAQ accordion but no structured data for it (missed rich snippet opportunity)
6. **No BreadcrumbList schema** — service pages show breadcrumbs visually but no structured data
7. **No Review/AggregateRating schema** — testimonials exist but no review schema for star ratings in search results

#### MEDIUM PRIORITY
8. **Massive Google Fonts request** — loading 25+ font families, hurting page speed
9. **OG image not dynamic per page** — same image used for all pages when shared on social media
10. **No `priceRange` in schema**
11. **Redundant Organization schema** (already covered by RoofingContractor)
